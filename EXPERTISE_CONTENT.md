## CMS Setup Checklist

1. ☐ Create new Page with slug `expertises`

3. ☐ Add "Expertise Domains" block
4. ☐ Add Section 1 (INDUSTRIE X.0) with 1 card
5. ☐ Add Section 2 (SOLUTIONS MÉTIER) with 3 cards
6. ☐ Add Section 3 (DATA & INTELLIGENCE) with 3 cards
7. ☐ Publish the page
